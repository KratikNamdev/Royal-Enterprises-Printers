Printing Solutions

U.V. Coating Machines and Conveyor

UV coating machines are equipment used in the printing industry to apply a special type of coating on paper, plastic, or other materials. The coating is cured by exposure to ultraviolet light, which results in a high-gloss finish that is resistant to scratches, abrasion, and fading.

Offset Printing Machines

Offset printing machines are a type of printing press used for high-volume commercial printing. They use a process known as offset lithography, which involves transferring an inked image from a printing plate to a rubber blanket, and then onto the paper or other substrate being printed.
